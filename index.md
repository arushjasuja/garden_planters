# Garden Planters

A strategic two-player board game where you move gardeners and plant flowers to outmaneuver your opponent. Play against an AI opponent or challenge a friend in this chess-meets-gardening strategy game!

## Game Modes

- **vs Computer** - Play against a strategic AI opponent
- **vs Player** - Play with a friend on the same device (hot-seat mode)

## Difficulty Levels

- **Beginner** - Sunflowers (15) and Rose Bushes (10) only - perfect for learning the basics
- **Standard** - All four flower types (Sunflowers, Roses, Lilies, Wildflowers) - the classic experience
- **Advanced** - All flowers plus special seasonal mechanics and garden features (Fountain, Compost Bin, Scarecrow)

## Advanced Level Features

**Four Seasons:**
- **Spring (Turns 1-8):** Normal gameplay
- **Summer (Turns 9-16):** Sunflowers block their square + 4 adjacent squares (up/down/left/right)
- **Autumn (Turns 17-24):** All flowers can be placed on FROM square OR any adjacent to destination
- **Winter (Turn 25+):** Gardeners can jump over Rose Bushes

## How to Play

**Each turn has 3 simple steps:**
1. **MOVE** - Select your gardener and move it like a chess queen (any straight line)
2. **PLANT** - Place a flower on the square you moved FROM
3. **END** - Your turn ends automatically
